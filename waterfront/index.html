What the data says

Why so much cheap waterfront right now: Hurricane Helene flooded the entire Pasco→Hernando→Citrus coast. That's why teardowns, gutted "investor special" canal homes, and pre-demolished lots are abundant and cheap — intact seawalls and docks, no house. Ideal for a pilings rebuild. The flip side: most of this inventory is VE or AE flood zone, so elevated construction is mandatory and flood insurance runs higher (VE can be $10K–$20K+/yr). For a new pilings build that's expected; for the math, budget it.
