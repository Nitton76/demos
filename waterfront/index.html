Why so much cheap waterfront right now: Hurricane Helene flooded the Pasco→Hernando→Citrus coast. That’s why teardowns, gutted canal homes, and pre-demolished lots are abundant and cheap — intact seawalls and docks, no house. Ideal for a pilings rebuild. The flip side: most of this inventory is VE or AE flood zone — elevated construction is mandatory. For a new pilings build that’s expected and actually lowers flood insurance vs. the old house.
